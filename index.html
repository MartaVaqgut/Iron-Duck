Duck Hunt
Tiro al pato by Ironhack!

HUNTER BLUE HUNTER RED


The game runs when you reload the page.
Player 1 (blue shoot) moves with the arrows and shoots with the shift.
Player 2 (red shoot) moves with the keys a, s, d, w and shoots with the r When you kill the duck (the pointer and the duck coincide) you score on the hunter.
At 10 points on the hunter, a warning of 'GAME OVER'
